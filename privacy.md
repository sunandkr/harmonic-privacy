# Privacy Policy                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                          
  **Last updated: January 23, 2026**                                                                                      
                                                                                                                          
  ## Overview                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                          
  Harmonic ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your   
  information.                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                          
  ## Data Collection & Storage                                                                                            
                                                                                                                          
  **All your data stays on your device.** Harmonic stores your check-ins, recovery activities, and preferences locally    
  using Apple's on-device storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.          
                                                                                                                          
  ## Third-Party Services                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                          
  Harmonic uses the Nager.Date API solely to fetch public holiday information for your selected country. No personal data 
  is shared with this service.                                                                                            
                                                                                                                          
  ## Data We Do NOT Collect                                                                                               
                                                                                                                          
  - Personal identification information                                                                                   
  - Location data                                                                                                         
  - Health data                                                                                                           
  - Usage analytics                                                                                                       
  - Advertising identifiers                                                                                               
                                                                                                                          
  ## Your Control                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                          
  You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app from your device.                                       
                                                                                                                          
  ## Children's Privacy                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                          
  Harmonic does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.                                                 
                                                                                                                          
  ## Changes to This Policy                                                                                               
                                                                                                                          
  We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
